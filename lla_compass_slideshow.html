Vision & Mission

Ways of Being

Communities in which all citizens are safe, happy, healthy, unafraid, prosperous, literate, and live powerfully in diverse, sustainable communities while experiencing sustained periods of well-being.

Ways of Doing

We exist to bridge the gap between life's potential and its daily realization, uniting members with shared interests in holistic well-being, ecological awareness, and the transformative power of artful living.

Our Purpose

This community seeks to empower individuals to live with intention and purpose, addressing the challenge of life's monotony and missed wonders.

We envision a world where individuals paint their lives with intention and purpose. Drawing from diverse human experiences and nature's wisdom, we aim to foster a community where choices prioritize personal growth and communal well-being.

Recognizing a pressing need for mindful living in an age of abundant opportunities, we aim to transform routine into enriching rituals. Inspired by art and literature, we champion conscious decision-making, turning routines into meaningful rituals.

Personal Outcomes

What members can expect to gain

  • Enhanced tools for managing change and uncertainty
  • Increased clarity and sensory awareness
  • Appreciation of literacy
  • Lifelong health, creativity, and emotional intelligence
  • Strengthened spirituality and relationships
  • Diverse educational and career opportunities
  • Financial and economic well-being
  • Environmental stewardship

Community Outcomes

What we aim to foster together

  • Robust civic engagement and responsibility
  • Environmental awareness and sustainability
  • Innovation and the creation of new programs, services, and products
  • A culture where diversity and inclusion are seen as assets
  • Enhanced community well-being and quality of life

Our Commitment

We are committed to understanding what it takes to be a good human being and citizen, and making informed decisions that benefit us individually, our natural world, our community, and our habitat.

Through this collective journey, we aspire to build harmonious communities that radiate harmony, gratitude, and purposeful living, celebrating diversity and sustainability at their core.
